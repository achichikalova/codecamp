Elizabeth I (1533-1603)

The 45-year reign of Queen Elizabeth I is heralded as a golden age in English history. The Elizabethan era witnessed the seafaring prowess of English adventurer Sir Francis Drake, great military defeat of the Spanish Armada, flourishing arts and drama written by Shakespeare and Marlowe, and the establishment of the modern-day Church of England.

"I know I have but the body of a weak and feeble woman, but I have the heart and stomach of a king, and of a king of England too."
